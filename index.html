Garden Stake Pool

About the project

The objective of this project was to create a Cardano stake pool (a 3rd generation blockchain technology). I led a team of 2 developers in order to deploy the stake pool on a Digital Ocean cloud server and develop a website. Our stake pool received 2.71 million worth of ADA (Cardano cryptocurrency) in delegation in just a few days. I currently maintain and operate the stake pool and its website.

Cloud Computing, Linux, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Git.

COVID-19 Simulation

About the project

As the outbreak of Coronavirus progressed, I noticed a lot of mentions of "exponential growth" in the media urging people to stay at home. It felt like people were grabbing on to this term without the adequate means to convey it to the general public. That's why I created a visual simulation of the outbreak in 2 scenarios - restricted movement and unrestricted movement.

Technologies used

Python, Matplotlib, Numpy, Resolve.

Unreal Webcam

About the project

A webapp that captures webcam display, draws it to a canvas, takes the pixel data out of the image, manipulates it and then draws it back. The effect applied is splitRGB combined with ghosting. The app also has a "take photo" functionality that captures images for downloading to local storage.

Technologies used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NPM.
